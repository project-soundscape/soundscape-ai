# Privacy Policy for SoundScape

**Last Updated**: February 3, 2026

## Introduction

SoundScape ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Audio Recordings
- **What**: Bird sound recordings you create
- **Why**: To identify bird species using AI
- **Storage**: Locally on device and optionally in cloud
- **Control**: You can delete recordings anytime

### 2. Location Data
- **What**: GPS coordinates when you record
- **Why**: To tag recordings with location for mapping
- **Storage**: Stored with each recording
- **Control**: You can disable location permission

### 3. Account Information (Optional)
- **What**: Email address if you create an account
- **Why**: To sync data across devices
- **Storage**: Secure cloud storage (Appwrite)
- **Control**: You can delete your account anytime

### 4. Device Information
- **What**: Device model, OS version
- **Why**: To optimize app performance
- **Storage**: Not stored long-term
- **Control**: Automatically collected, anonymized

## How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:
- Identify bird species in your recordings
- Tag recordings with geographic location
- Sync your data across devices (if signed in)
- Improve our AI identification accuracy
- Provide customer support

## Data Storage & Security

### Local Storage
- Recordings stored in app-specific directory
- Protected by device security (PIN, biometrics)

### Cloud Storage
- Encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
- Stored on Appwrite servers (EU)
- Access controlled by your credentials

### Speech Detection
- We detect human speech in recordings
- Recordings with speech are flagged
- This protects your privacy from accidental voice capture

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:
- Sell your personal information
- Share data with advertisers
- Use data for targeted advertising

We MAY share:
- Anonymized data for research (with consent)
- Data required by law enforcement (with valid legal request)

## Third-Party Services

Our app uses:
- **Appwrite**: Cloud backend (see their [privacy policy](https://appwrite.io/privacy))
- **OpenStreetMap**: Map tiles (no personal data sent)
- **Wikipedia API**: Species information (no personal data sent)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:
- **Access**: View all your stored data
- **Delete**: Remove your recordings and account
- **Export**: Download your data
- **Opt-out**: Use app offline without cloud sync

## Children's Privacy

SoundScape is rated for ages 13+. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## Data Retention

- **Local data**: Retained until you delete it
- **Cloud data**: Retained until you delete account
- **Account deletion**: Data removed within 30 days

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted in-app and on our website. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.
